About Bulb Vine

Your trusted partner in bringing greenery and life to every space

Nurturing Nature, One Plant at a Time

Founded by Gesandu Amarasinghe, Bulb Vine is a premium plant nursery dedicated to providing the finest selection of plant bulbs, vine plants, and indoor/outdoor greenery. We believe that every space deserves a touch of nature.

Our commitment to quality, freshness, and eco-friendly service sets us apart. Each plant is carefully nurtured and selected to ensure it thrives in its new home, bringing beauty and tranquility to your living spaces.
